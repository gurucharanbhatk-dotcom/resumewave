How it works

How ResumeWave
works

ResumeWave uses Claude AI (by Anthropic) with India-specific prompts that understand Naukri's ATS rules, Indian resume formats, and what recruiters at Indian companies search for.

STEP 1 — You enter your details
Fill in your skills, experience, role, and target companies. For ATS checking, paste your resume text and the job description.
STEP 2 — AI processes your input
Your input is sent to Claude AI through a secure proxy. The AI uses India-specific rules: Naukri RChilli parser requirements, correct "Mon Year" date formats, minimum 10 skills for Naukri completeness score, and quantified bullet patterns.
STEP 3 — You get your result
ATS score with specific fixes, or a complete resume in plain text format. Copy it, paste into Word/Google Docs, and upload to Naukri or LinkedIn.
STEP 4 — Apply with confidence
Your resume is formatted to pass Naukri's RChilli parser, appear in recruiter searches, and survive ATS filters at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and most Indian MNCs.
Is my data safe?

Your resume text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate results. We do not store your resume, personal details, or any input after your session ends. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy. We never sell or share your data.

What AI does it use?

ResumeWave is powered by Claude Sonnet (by Anthropic) — one of the leading AI models for writing and analysis tasks. The results are AI-generated and should be reviewed by you before submitting applications. Always verify the output is accurate for your situation.
