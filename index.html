How it works

Three steps to financial clarity

No manual entry, no templates, no complicated setup. Just upload and get your complete financial picture.

1

Upload your statement

Drag and drop your bank statement in any format — CSV, Excel, PDF, or OFX. We detect the format automatically and skip any customer info rows at the top.

2

We read and classify it

Our smart engine finds dates, amounts, and descriptions automatically. Every transaction gets categorised — income, travel, software, food, and more.

3

See your full picture

Income smoothing, tax estimates, expense breakdown, cash flow forecast, and AI-powered insights — all in one clear dashboard.

To provide financial analysis, tax estimates, and dashboard features. The AI Analyst sends only an anonymised summary (totals and categories) to Claude API — never individual transaction descriptions.
