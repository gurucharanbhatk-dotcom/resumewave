How it works

How ResumeWave
works

ResumeWave uses a built-in recruiter keyword engine that understands Naukri ATS rules, LinkedIn HR selection criteria, and what hiring managers at global MNCs — including finance, consulting, and tech firms — actually search for when filtering candidates.

STEP 1 — You enter your details
Fill in your skills, experience, role, and target companies. For ATS checking, paste your resume text and the job description.
STEP 2 — AI processes your input
Your input is processed through our secure resume engine. It applies industry-specific rules: Naukri RChilli parser requirements, correct "Mon Year" date formats, minimum 10 skills for Naukri completeness score, and quantified bullet patterns.
STEP 3 — You get your result
ATS score with specific fixes, or a complete resume in plain text format. Copy it, paste into Word/Google Docs, and upload to Naukri or LinkedIn.
STEP 4 — Apply with confidence
Your resume is formatted to pass Naukri's RChilli parser, appear in recruiter searches, and survive ATS filters at global MNCs — Deloitte, KPMG, Amazon, JP Morgan, Google, Accenture and more.
Is my data safe?

Your resume text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate results. We do not store your resume, personal details, or any input after your session ends. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy. We never sell or share your data.

How does the keyword engine work?

ResumeWave's keyword engine is trained on recruiter search patterns from Naukri, LinkedIn, and job descriptions across global MNCs in finance, consulting, technology, and operations. Results are suggestions — always review and personalise before submitting your application.
